Welcome! I have been working on HopperHawk on and off in my (limited) free time for a while now, and have finally reached a point where I can share it with others!

Version 1.0

Specifications

  • Based on the ESP32
  • Support for an 18650 Lithium Ion battery
  • Uses the TOF10120 sensor for measurements
  • Has a BCM to support charging/load sharing
  • Includes two onboard LEDs to indicate power and charging status
  • USB-C port for power
  • Headers for USB TTL communication (to flash) and to put into BOOT mode

Background

HopperHawk started as a project for myself, and I never intended on releasing it out into the world.
