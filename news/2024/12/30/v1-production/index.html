First batch currently in production!

News December 30, 2024

✎ Edit Post

“Good news everyone!” the first batch of HopperHawk v1.0 is now in production! Once assembly/testing is complete at the factory I will update my stock/inventory on Tindie. Be sure to order quickly! Round one is limited to 10 units (based on pre-orders and interest through Reddit).

If you haven’t already, sign up for the waitlist on Tindie! As soon as the stock is available you’ll receive an email and a chance to order. The base unit which includes the board and sensor will cost $50. You’ll be able to add the case for an additional $10 (which also includes free assembly). All units will be flashed and ready to setup once you receive them!

If you have any questions, be sure to reach out: joe@sidelinedata.com
